Instructions

The exercise will consist on simple mathematical addition. In a sequence of three screens, you will be shown three independent numbers, one at a time.

Your job will be to simply add “X” to each of those numbers. “X” will vary depending one whether you choose the easy or the difficult task. In the easy task, you will always be asked to add “0” to the number. In the difficult task, the number will be either 1, 3, or 7.

At the end of the three screens, you will be shown a correct answer and an incorrect answer to the addition. Choose the correct answer to secure the credits!
